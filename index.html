Dr. Sindhutai Sapkal
Dr. Sindhutai Sapkal, also known as the "Mother of Orphans"

Dr. Sindhutai Sapkal, also known as the "Mother of Orphans", is a social worker and activist from India who hasdedicated her life to the care and education of orphaned and destitute children.

In 1970, Dr. Sapkal started taking in orphaned children and providing them with a home, food, and education. Over the years, she has raised and cared for over 1,400 children and has established numerous schools, orphanages, and care homes for the underprivileged.

Her tireless work and dedication to the cause of child welfare has earned her numerous accolades,including the Padma Shri award from the Government of India in 2008. Despite her achievements, she remains humble and continues to work for the betterment of society.
